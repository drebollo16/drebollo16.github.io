Target Audience webpage

The targeted audience is anyone no matter their age group. Who are wanting to know the weather outcome, for the weeks and able to get the correct results. Outdoor, indoor, schools, construction sites, and all different types of business needs for weather updates.

Two persona

  • Person One "Kate"

    • She is an outdoor person, who works for a new broading casting coming. She is in her 30's and enjoys spending her weekends doing out door activities. Hiking, running, swimming and taking her dogs for walks. She like to be able to keep track of her outdoor progress by going to the Night star weather app for accurate information. If she needs to prepare or cancel plans. The website will be able to help a person like kate to continue with her daily routine and give her week, month notice of possible weather outcome.


  • Persona two "John"

    • He is a winter outdoor activity person and also enjoys the summer activites. He works for a accounting company, is in his 40's, he is planning start his own business. When he is not working he loves to travel and enjoys the out doors from fishing, biking, swimming and running. He always wait for the weather reports and dresses accordingly. With the activties he does he relies on technology to give him updates about the weather forecast for the week.
