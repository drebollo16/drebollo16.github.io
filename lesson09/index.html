Annual Star Gazing event

The 10th Annual star Gazing event,,will be held at the fields from 8pm - 11pm. The weather predictions for July 10th 2020 is clear skies and warmer temperatures in the evenings. Bring your telescopes and your blankets. Star gazing is a family fun activity that people of all ages are welcome to attend and no one is left out. We will be providing you with refreshments. We hope you are all able to make it and enjoy a night out with family and friends the community.
