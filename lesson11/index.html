Welcome to our Weather Site!

Welcome to the Night star Weather, where we report accurate weather reporting, town updates and news updates. We been established since 2020, We are the one stop for all your weather needs. We currently are reporting on three counties, which are Preston Idaho located in Franklin County. Soda Springs, Idaho which is located in Caribu County. The other city we report on is Fish Haven, Idaho which is located in Bear Lake county. Coming to our site you will be able to get all the weather needs, we will provide you with a 5 day forecast for all you planning needs.
